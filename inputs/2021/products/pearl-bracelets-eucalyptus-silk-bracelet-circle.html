Eucalyptus Silk Bracelet - Circle

Inspired by the nautical ropes used on our pearling ships, this contemporary design features a detachable semi-round Paspaley Pearl, allowing you to tailor your look with different coloured silks and pearls in the collection. The bracelet is available in two sizes and can be adjusted to suit your wrist by knotting the silk and using the slider to secure the pearl.
Paspaley Bracelet in 18kt yellow gold featuring one detachable, 10-12mm circlé pearl in Eucalyptus silk. Small max length 18.5cm Large max length 20cm
